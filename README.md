## What is DOH Money?

DOH Money is a **professional-grade crypto trading terminal** built as a native desktop app (and web app at [app.doh.money](https://app.doh.money)). It aggregates live data from **22 centralised exchanges** and the **Alephium blockchain**, giving you a unified view of the entire market from one screen.

---

## Features

### 📊 Markets Dashboard
*Global multi-exchange terminal covering 22 CEXs*

![Markets Dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dohmoney/releases/main/screenshots/markets-dashboard.png)

- **Live ticker bar** — real-time prices for over 4200+ tokens + TradFi assets (aggregated across all exchanges)
- **NYSE market status** — live open/closed/pre-market/after-hours badge with countdown timer
- **NYSE Opening Bell** — synthesized bell plays automatically at 9:30 AM ET
- **Token list** with tabs: Hot · Gainers · Losers · Top · Arb · New · Favorites
- **Price flash animation** — rows flash green/red on every tick
- **TradingView Advanced Chart** — auto-selects best exchange feed (Binance → Bybit → OKX → MEXC fallback)
- **Live Trades Feed** — real-time buy/sell stream with whale detection
- **Order Book + Depth Chart** — combined bid/ask ladders with cumulative depth visualization
- **Cross-exchange Arbitrage** — live spread scanner for every tracked token
- **Futures data** — funding rates, open interest, and funding arb across Bybit · OKX · Gate · Binance
- **Risk Report** — AI-style rug/dump risk scoring with live alerts
- **Journal** — private per-session trading notes
- **News Feed** — live/realtime news feed across different categories that impact financial markets
- **Nostr feed** — decentralized crypto social timeline with feed and chatrooms per token
- **TV tab** — CryptoWatch YouTube video feed + TradingView full-screen chart
- **X/Twitter feeds** — multi-column or merged timeline for crypto accounts

**Right sidebar** with 12 independently collapsible, drag-to-reorder sections (right or left sidebar) - fully customizaable:  
Live TV · Token Favorites rotator · Rug Alerts · Token Details · Market Pulse · Futures · Fear & Greed · Top Movers · Latest News · X Feeds · Market Hours · Arbitrage

---

### ⛓️ Alephium Dashboard
*Native on-chain DEX analytics terminal*

![Alephium Dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dohmoney/releases/main/screenshots/alephium-dashboard.png)

- **TradingView chart** + custom OHLCV candle chart (Lightweight Charts) for all DEX pools
- **Real-time on-chain transactions** — every DEX trade, progressive all-time history load
- **Whale detection** — large trades highlighted with distinct styling
- **TVL history chart** — total value locked over time per pool
- **Trader leaderboard** — top traders, largest positions, volume leaders
- **Token holder distribution** — full holder list + ALPH richlist + Connected Wallets
- **CEX inflow tracker** — real-time ALPH transfers to/from exchange hot wallets
- **Wallet Tracker** — full on-chain portfolio analytics, P&L, and transaction history for any address
- **Deep-link support** — every view bookmarkable: `/alephium?tab=explorer&wallet=<address>`
- **Supported DEXs**: Elexium · Linx Labs · Nightshade · and more

---

### 📈 Arbitrage Dashboard
*Cross-exchange spread scanner and bot simulator*

![Arbitrage Dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dohmoney/releases/main/screenshots/arbitrage-dashboard.png)

- Scans **22 exchanges** realtime for live price discrepancies
- Net profit modelling after exchange fees, slippage, and latency
- Full **bot simulator** — configure capital, route, fees, and simulate execution
- Supported exchanges: MEXC · Gate.io · Bybit · Binance · OKX · Bitget · Coinbase · KuCoin · HTX · Kraken · Gemini · Crypto.com · CoinEx · BitMart · Bitstamp · Bitfinex · Upbit · Phemex · LBank · XT.com · Pionex · Biconomy

## Auto-Updates

The desktop app checks for new releases automatically on launch. When an update is available:

1. It downloads silently in the background
2. A notification appears when ready
3. Click **Restart Now** to install — done

No manual re-downloading needed.
